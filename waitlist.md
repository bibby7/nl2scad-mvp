# Waitlist Flow

## Goal
Collect qualified early users, learn their use cases, and invite in batches.

## Fields
- Name
- Email
- Role (hobbyist / designer / print shop)
- What do you want to build? (short text)

## Confirmation Email Copy
**Subject:** You’re on the NL → OpenSCAD waitlist

Hey {{first_name}},

Thanks for signing up. We’re inviting small batches soon. If you reply with one part you want to build, we’ll prioritize your invite.

— The NL → OpenSCAD team
